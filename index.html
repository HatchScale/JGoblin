Privacy Policy for JGoblin

Last Updated: January 2026

TL;DR: JGoblin stores your resume and API keys locally on your device. We do not own servers, and we never see or sell your data.

1. Information We Collect

To provide its core functionality, JGoblin processes the following data:

  • Personally Identifiable Information (PII): Professional details from your resume (name, email, work history).
  • Authentication Information: Your personal AI API keys (OpenAI or Gemini) used to power the assistant.
  • Website Content: Text you manually select from job applications to generate answers.

2. How Data is Handled

All data is stored locally in your browser's storage. We do not collect or store your data on any external servers. Data is only transmitted directly to your chosen AI provider (OpenAI or Gemini) via their official APIs to generate your content.

3. Third-Party Services

When you use the extension, your data is processed by the AI service provider you configured. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of OpenAI or Google Gemini.

4. Your Rights

Since all data is local, you have full control. You can delete your resume or API keys at any time by clearing the extension's storage or uninstalling the extension.

5. Contact

For questions, contact the developer via the Chrome Web Store support page.
